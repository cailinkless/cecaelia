Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell the things I make from your patterns?

Yes, if you credit me for the design! Verbiage can be as simple as "Design by Cecaelia Creations," with a link to the specific pattern page (when online) or www.cecaelia.com (for printed materials).

Can I sell your patterns?

Nope, sorry!

Can I share your patterns?

Yes, by linking back to the pattern on this website. Please do not reproduce my patterns in any other way (including producing tutorials). Thanks!

What if I find an error in a pattern?

Shoot me a message through my ravelry account and I'll get back to you ASAP!
